We fight because we believe that

The sex industry today is a global system of violence and exploitation, primarily targeting women and children.

It's made up of those who are coerced and forced into it—what we call "sex trafficking"—as well as those who have been compelled to sell their bodies in order to survive. It includes those who are exploited in prostitution, pornography, and stripping. The sex industry preys on our society’s most vulnerable ones, all to profit pimps, traffickers, and other predatory stakeholders.

Life shouldn’t be this way for any person.

Together, we can break the cycle of sexual exploitation and bring freedom to the captives.
